About

This is the story of a girl living the full human experience. The anxiety, depression, happiness, beauty, love, loneliness, and everything that comes with it. This is the journal of a girl who wasn’t supposed to make it past 18, but here she is. Sharing her feelings, her art, and the little things that make her happy. The little things that helped her grow.

She wants you to know that you’re not alone .

Who is Eros ?

Eros is the greek god of love. Self love has been one of the biggest struggles in the human experience. Dream of Eros represents the ultimate goal of learning to love yourself and others. Learning to love your life and the meaning behind what makes you who you are.

Whether your'e evil by design or pure of heart, you should know you are special.
